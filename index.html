We are excited to announce the

1st Annual

ALPHARETTA POP FEST

which will be held at Brooke Street Park in downtown Alpharetta on Saturday, June 16th, 2018. The festival will be a free, family-friendly event to raise money and awareness for Alzheimer's Disease.

About the ALPHARETTA POP FEST

  • Event will start at 2pm with a Children's Music Education Show

    featuring "The Sandels Music Party"

  • "Pop Alley" will be located in the gardens beside Alpharetta City Hall

    featuring, Food Truck: Smoking Mo's & King of Pops

  • Wine tastings provided by

    Vino 100/Tinder Box & Kaya Vineyards

  • Beer tastings provided by Back Forty Brewing

  • Main Stage will be open from 3pm - 9:30pm

    featuring artists including:

    • Sailing to Denver
    • The Hot Hearts
    • Jive Revival
    • Fat Arm Daddy
    • Mariela
